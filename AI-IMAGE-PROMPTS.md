## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS FOR ALL IMAGES

### General Guidelines:
1. **Resolution:** Minimum 1200px width for hero images, 1000px for regular images
2. **Format:** JPEG or PNG
3. **Color Profile:** sRGB
4. **Lighting:** Natural, warm lighting preferred
5. **Composition:** Rule of thirds, eye-level shots for children
6. **Safety:** All images should show safe, supervised activities

### Cultural Authenticity:
- Kerala features for children (medium to dark skin tones, black hair)
- Traditional Kerala elements subtly incorporated
- Modern educational environment with local cultural respect
- Malayalam language elements where appropriate (posters, signs)
- Traditional and modern clothing blend

### Diversity Representation:
- Mix of boys and girls
- Various skin tones within Kerala/Indian spectrum
- Inclusive activities
- No stereotyping

### Safety & Ethics:
- All children supervised
- Safe equipment and materials
- No choking hazards visible
- Appropriate clothing
- Respectful, dignified representation

## PROMPT TIPS FOR BETTER RESULTS

1. **Be Specific:** Include age, clothing, activity, setting details
2. **Cultural Context:** Always mention "Kerala" or "Indian" for authentic representation
3. **Lighting:** Specify "natural lighting" or "soft natural sunlight"
4. **Style:** Add "photorealistic," "professional photography," or "documentary style"
5. **Safety Elements:** Include "supervised," "safe environment," "rounded edges"
6. **Negative Prompts:** Use to exclude: "cartoon, illustration, artificial, studio lighting, western setting"

## IMAGE REPLACEMENT GUIDE

### In HTML Files:
1. Find the image tag: `<img src="images/placeholder.jpg">`
2. Replace with generated image: `<img src="images/hero-classroom.jpg">`
3. Update alt text to describe the actual image content

### File Naming Convention:
- hero-[section].jpg
- playgroup-[activity].jpg
- nursery-[activity].jpg
- preprimary-[activity].jpg
- outdoor-[location].jpg
- classroom-[type].jpg
